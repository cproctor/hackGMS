hackGMS is a program designed by middle-schoolers to change the way our school—the Girls' Middle School—works.

In the words of our fearless leader, Chris Proctor:

We will create a “message board” web application for daily announcements, to be displayed on screens around the school. Parents will be able to send a text while parked in the traffic line to alert girls to meet their ride.

For more information on the work that will go into the project, check out our Github repository!
